What you see here is me, Kyle, performing the third movement of Beethoven's Piano Sonata No. 8 in C minor, Op. 13 ("Pathétique"), Rondo: Allegro. It's a complicated piece; the rhythm and melodies are rapid, with many twists and turns and changes in mood. All this is in the finest tradition of Beethoven. You may hear my recording as an attempt to capture the mood and brilliance of this piano masterpiece.
